Tarot House Support

Tarot House is a compact three-card workspace for tarot readers. This page provides help with common questions and technical problems.

Contact support

Email: sliepovv@gmail.com

When reporting a problem, please include your Chrome version, the website where the issue occurred, and a short description of what happened.

The extension does not open

  • Refresh the current webpage.
  • Click the Tarot House toolbar icon again.
  • Check that the extension is enabled in Chrome.
  • Tarot House cannot run on Chrome internal pages or on the Chrome Web Store.

The panel is outside the visible area

Open Settings and select Restore default layout. This returns the panel to its default position and Standard size.

Card images do not appear

Refresh the webpage and reopen Tarot House. All card images are stored locally inside the extension and do not require an account.

Copy cards does not work

Reveal at least one card first. Copy cards copies only the names of currently revealed cards.
